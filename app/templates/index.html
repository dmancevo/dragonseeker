  • Villagers: Know the secret word. Must identify and eliminate the Dragon.
  • Knights: Know a similar (but different) word. Don't know they are knights - they think they are villagers! Must identify and eliminate the Dragon.
  • Dragon: Doesn't know the word! Must blend in and avoid being caught.

ðŸŽ¯ Objective

  • Villagers/Knights win if: They successfully vote out the Dragon
  • Dragon wins if: They survive until only 2 players remain, OR correctly guess the word after being eliminated

ðŸŽ® Game Flow

  1. Host creates a game and shares the link with friends
  2. Players join and enter their nicknames
  3. Host starts the game (minimum 3 players)
  4. Each player receives their role and secret word (or blank for Dragon)
  5. Players discuss to figure out who the Dragon is
  6. Host initiates voting when ready
  7. All players vote for who they think is the Dragon
  8. Player with most votes is eliminated
  9. If Dragon is eliminated, they get one chance to guess the word
  10. Game continues until a winner is determined
Tip: Best played in person or over voice chat for maximum fun!
